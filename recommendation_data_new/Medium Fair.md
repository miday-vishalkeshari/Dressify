Medium Fair (Olive/Beige)
Best Colors:

Earthy tones (terracotta, olive green, mustard)

Warm pinks, burnt orange, mauve

Denim blue, turquoise, lavender

Neutrals: Warm browns, sand, oatmeal.

Avoid: Overly ashy tones (can dull warmth).
